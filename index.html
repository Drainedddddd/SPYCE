New to Crypto? Here's How to Get Started!

Excited about SPYCE but new to the world of cryptocurrency? No worries, we've got you! To get SPYCE tokens and participate in our ecosystem, you'll first need some cryptocurrency. Here’s a simple guide:

1. Get Crypto with an On-Ramp Service

Services called "on-ramps" make it super easy to buy popular cryptocurrencies (like Ethereum, Bitcoin, or stablecoins) using your regular bank card, Apple Pay, or other familiar payment methods. They are designed to be user-friendly, even for complete beginners!

A great and widely trusted option is MoonPay. They offer a straightforward way to purchase crypto.

Buy Crypto on MoonPay

Other popular on-ramps include Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken, depending on your region.

2. Set Up Your Secure Crypto Wallet

Once you've bought your crypto, you'll need a secure digital "wallet" to store it and interact with Web3 platforms like SPYCE. Think of it as your personal account in the crypto world.

For SPYCE, you'll need a compatible Web3 wallet. Details on specific supported wallets will be announced soon. Generally, these are browser extensions or mobile apps. Popular examples include:

  • MetaMask (very popular for Ethereum and EVM-compatible chains)
  • Phantom (popular for Solana-based projects)
  • Trust Wallet (versatile mobile wallet)

Always download wallets from their official websites and keep your recovery phrase (seed phrase) extremely safe and private!

With crypto in your secure wallet, you're all set! When SPYCE launches, you'll be able to connect your wallet to our platform, become eligible for the airdrop based on your holdings, and start enjoying the unique benefits of being a SPYCE community member.
